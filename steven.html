ABOUT STEVEN JONES

Head Coach & Founder

Steven Jones is the visionary behind Iron Sight Coaching, a premier bodybuilding coaching brand dedicated to transforming athletes' physiques through science-based training and nutrition methodologies.

With over a decade of competitive bodybuilding experience and coaching expertise, Steven has built Iron Sight Coaching from the ground up into a respected brand known for delivering exceptional results to serious athletes.

The Iron Sight Philosophy

Iron Sight Coaching represents more than just training - it's a comprehensive approach to physique development that combines precise nutrition, strategic training, and mental conditioning. The name "Iron Sight" reflects the brand's commitment to precision, focus, and targeted results.

As both the founder and head coach, Steven personally oversees every client's journey, ensuring that the Iron Sight standard of excellence is maintained across all coaching programs.
